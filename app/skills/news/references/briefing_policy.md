# Briefing Policy

Use this reference when the user asks for a daily or recent AI news briefing.

- Summarize visible list-page items into concise Chinese.
- Include the title, source, link, topic label, and a short reason each item matters.
- Distinguish papers from blog posts.
- Say when source reading is unavailable or failed.
- Do not imply that full papers or full articles were read when only list pages were available.
